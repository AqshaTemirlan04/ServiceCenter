Rules of the Hub Community

Openness
The Service center is open to everyone. Also, the openness of the community implies the openness of the internal activities of the community. Meeting of Leads, development and planning of community development is held in an open manner with the community itself.
Equality of opportunity
There is a minimal hierarchy within our service center.Each new employee will have their own mentors who are moderators and representatives of their service center and help in the formation of subgroups, the settlement of internal conflicts.It is the leading specialists who organize the joint activities of several specialists.
Freedom
Freedom of activity - in the first place for each member of the community, his own Personality is put. No one in the community has the right to oblige anyone. The participant always has the right to refuse any activity, or to leave the community altogether at any time.
Decentralization
Community administration is based on the principles of decentralization. The HUB is divided into several specialized, junior Hubs, each of which works independently of the others and is coordinated by a maximum of two Leads. The internal policy of each of the junior Hubs is determined by its Leads.
